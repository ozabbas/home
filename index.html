About me

Born and raised in the great state of Texas. I attended the University of Houston in pursuit of a Nutrition Degree and became a certified personal trainer. Fitness has always been one of my passions in life especially when I was able to use my knowledge to help others better themselves. After a few years of working in an emergency room as a Scribe and dealing with new software frequently, I became interested in coding and the challenges it brought with it. This is when I decided to attend Actualize Coding Bootcamp to learn more about becoming a web developer. Coding has since developed into one of my favorite pastimes. In particular, I enjoy working on independent projects and building apps with the knowledge I have gained.
